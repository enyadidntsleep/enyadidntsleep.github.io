How to make it?

1. Crack an egg, then add 75 ml of coffee, 1 teaspoon of vanilla essence, and add a tablespoon of brown sugar. Or you can use cinnamon powder instead of brown sugar.
2. Stir until smooth or until the sugar is dissolved, then dip the bread. Both surfaces of the bread must be wet to penetrate the coffee flavor.
3. After that, heat the pan and give a little butter so it doesn't stick, then place the bread on the pan.
4. Toast the bread for about 4-5 minutes, then turn the bread over until it is evenly cooked.
5. When it is cooked, place it neatly on a plate. Add with any toppings you like, I use cream cheese and powdered milk chocolate.
6. Eat wile it's warm. Enjoy!
